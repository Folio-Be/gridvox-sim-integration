# GridVox Livery Builder - Community Features & Gamification

**Document Purpose:** Comprehensive specification of community-driven features, credit economy, marketplace, social features, and gamification systems that make GridVox Livery Builder a thriving ecosystem.

**Date:** November 12, 2025  
**Vision:** Build a self-sustaining community where contributors are rewarded and users are engaged

---

## Community Philosophy

### Core Tenets

1. **Creators First:** Reward those who contribute value
2. **Earn, Don't Pay:** Make earning credits easier than buying them
3. **Quality Over Quantity:** Incentivize high-quality contributions
4. **Social by Design:** Community features integrated, not bolted on
5. **Transparent Economy:** Clear credit earning/spending rules

---

## Credit Economy System

### Philosophy: "Contribute to Earn"

Unlike traditional subscription-only models, GridVox rewards community contribution. The more you help the community, the more credits you earn. Credits can be used for premium features, making the platform accessible to all, regardless of financial means.

### Starting Credits

**New User Welcome Package:**
- 10,000 credits upon signup (enough for first month)
- 100 credits per month (free tier allowance)
- Bonus 5,000 credits for completing onboarding tutorial
- Bonus 2,000 credits for first livery upload to community

**Total First Month:** 17,100 credits (enough to explore premium features)

#### Liveries
- **Base Upload:** 10 credits (just for uploading)

- **Per 100 Downloads:** 100 credits bonus
- **1,000 Downloads:** 500 credits bonus

- **Write Helpful Review:** 5 credits (max 3/day)

**Validation Criteria:**
- Minimum 50 characters

## Credit Withdrawal (Cash Out)

**Creator Monetization:**

Users can convert earned credits to real money:
- **Rate:** 1,000 credits = $5 USD
- **Minimum Withdrawal:** 5,000 credits ($25)
- **Processing Fee:** 5%
- **Payment Methods:** PayPal, bank transfer, Stripe
- **Payout Schedule:** Monthly or on-demand (min $25)

**GridVox Commission:**
- 50% on credit sales (marketplace)
- 30% on template/graphic sales
- 0% on earned credits (rewards for contributing)

**Example:**
- User earns 10,000 credits from popular liveries (free contributions)
- User sells template for 1,000 credits √ó 20 times = 20,000 credits (earns 70% = 14,000 credits)
- **Total earned:** 24,000 credits
- **Withdrawable:** 24,000 credits = $120 (minus 5% fee = $114)
